About Us

A Legacy of Luxury & Hospitality

Nestled on the pristine shores of a private island in Fiji, Sunrise Resort has been crafting unforgettable experiences since 1998. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has made us a preferred destination for discerning travelers worldwide.

From the moment you arrive, our dedicated team ensures every aspect of your stay exceeds expectations. With world-class amenities, gourmet dining, and breathtaking natural beauty, we create memories that last a lifetime.
